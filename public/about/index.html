A little About Sparkonics

Welcome to Sparkonics, the premier electrical club of IIT Patna. We are a dynamic community of passionate electrical engineering students and enthusiasts who are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of innovation and technology.

At Sparconics, we believe in the power of collaboration and hands-on learning. Our team comprises brilliant minds who are constantly exploring cutting-edge technologies in electrical engineering, electronics, robotics, and power systems. Especially Digital VLSI.

Our club serves as a platform for students to transform theoretical knowledge into practical applications. We organize workshops, seminars, and technical competitions that challenge our members to think creatively and solve real-world problems. Through various projects ranging from IoT implementations to renewable energy systems, we foster an environment of continuous learning and innovation.

Join us on our journey as we illuminate the future with our electrical engineering prowess and continue to spark innovation in the field of technology at IIT Patna.

Frontend Development

Our frontend development process focuses on creating exceptional user experiences through modern web technologies and best practices. We leverage the power of HTML5 semantic markup to ensure accessibility and search engine optimization, while utilizing the latest CSS3 features including flexbox, grid layouts, and custom properties for maintainable styling systems.

JavaScript ES6+ forms the backbone of our interactive applications, enabling us to build dynamic, responsive interfaces that adapt seamlessly across all devices. We implement modern architectural patterns such as component-based design and state management to ensure scalable and maintainable codebases that can evolve with changing requirements.

Performance optimization is at the core of our development philosophy. We utilize advanced techniques such as lazy loading, code splitting, and progressive enhancement to deliver lightning-fast user experiences. Our commitment to web standards and progressive web app technologies ensures that our applications work reliably across all browsers and platforms.

Backend Development

Our backend architecture is built on PHP 8.2, The reason for using php is our servers very optimised for php, the main handler is apache, and hence the choices, to create high-performance server-side applications. We design RESTful APIs endpoints that provide seamless data communication between frontend applications and database systems, ensuring optimal performance and developer experience.

We have build our own MVC architecture ensuring lesser foot-print of code, and highly customisation to meet our hardware requirements.

Database design and management are crucial aspects of our backend development process. We worked with sqlite db for its high read speed and its low requirements storage. Our database architectures are optimized for scalability and Every page has its own db, ensuring we won’t have to worry about file locking.

Security and reliability are paramount in our backend systems. We implement comprehensive authentication and authorization systems, data validation, rate limiting, and monitoring solutions. We have make sure to take care of sql injections, session management, and proper encryption of the session ids.

We offered admin panel, to access the backend server, and gave different powers for cords and subcoords. We have build a scheduler using PHPMAILER for automated remainder and planner. We have our own community specific messaging system, build on openswoole. (Due to hardware constraints, we weren’t able to find solutions)
